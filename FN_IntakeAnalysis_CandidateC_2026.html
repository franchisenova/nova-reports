On the call Acknowledge the 6-month urgency without dismissing it. Frame it as "let's move fast on the capital modeling so we compress the timeline as much as possible — the 4-unit path requires a few extra weeks of structuring that a single-unit deal doesn't."

  • Are there any franchise systems you've already been in conversations with, or that you've ruled out and why?
