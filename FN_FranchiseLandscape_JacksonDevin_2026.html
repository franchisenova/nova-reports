About This List

Based on the sectors you selected and your target geography, we put together a starting list of franchise systems worth exploring as you continue your journey with Franchise Nova. These systems were identified based on publicly available information — their territory availability, revenue model, ownership structure, and fit with your goals.

This is a starting point for your own research and conversation with your Nova Guide — not a formal recommendation. Any system you want to explore further will go through Franchise Nova's full FDD analysis process before any decisions are made.

Est. Investment = total estimated cost to open one location (franchise fee + build-out + equipment + working capital) — not what you earn. B2C = your customers are individual consumers. B2B = your customers are other businesses.

⚑ Public information only — no FDDs reviewed. Territory availability must be confirmed directly with each franchisor before proceeding.
