How a candidate moves through the
Franchise Nova pipeline.

From first intake to franchise analysis, every candidate follows the same path — sorted into a track, guided through their discovery call, and once they sign, moved into the deal pipeline stage by stage. Here's the full journey, and who does what along the way.

1

Intake arrives

A new candidate submits their intake. The team is notified right away so nothing sits unseen.

Automatic Slack · #fn_candidate_activity
2

Run the analysis

Brenda runs the intake through the Claude prompt. When it finishes, two files land in the #fn_candidate_activity Slack channel: the Internal Analysis for the team, and the Landscape Analysis — shared with the candidate before their kickoff call.

Both links are saved to the candidate's HubSpot notes, and the landscape link is written to the contact's Landscape URL property.

Brenda HubSpot note Property · Landscape URL
3

Validate the analysis & track

The team reviews the intake analysis, validates what it's showing, and confirms the candidate's track — SuperNova, Nova, or Nebula. Once it's settled, Mel sets the track on the record.

Team Mel Property · FN Track
4

Invite to book the discovery call

An automatic email — matched to the candidate's track — invites them to book. Every candidate gets a discovery call; booking runs through Mel's Google booking link.

If they don't book, an automatic nurture sequence keeps reaching out until they do — the cadence differs by track, shown below.

Automatic Booking · Mel's Google link

5

The call gets booked

HubSpot updates on its own: the Discovery Call Outcome becomes Scheduled and the date/time is saved. Reschedule and it updates; cancel and the date/time clears and the outcome flips to Cancelled.

Automatic Property · Discovery Call Outcome Property · Discovery Call Date
6

The discovery call

Where we get to know the candidate. The internal analysis includes suggested questions to dig deeper — the goal is to validate what the analysis is showing and decide whether they're a fit. At the end, the Nova Guide marks the outcome.

Nova Guide Property · Discovery Call Outcome → Completed / No Show

→ Accepted

A welcome email goes out automatically. The Nova Guide generates the PandaDoc participation agreement and sends it to the candidate and Chipo to sign.

Once signed, the fields update, a deal is created, and a Slack alert fires.

✦ Now a Deal — Post-Agreement
8

Agreement signed → kickoff

The signed agreement automatically creates the deal and moves the candidate from Contact to Deal in HubSpot, and a Slack alert lets the team know. The Nova Guide and team prepare for the kickoff call.

Before the kickoff, the candidate receives their Landscape analysis (the link on their profile under Landscape URL) to review and bring questions. On the call, they come prepared with a few franchise systems they're interested in — the goal is to understand their thinking and help them narrow toward what fits their goals.

Automatic Deal created Slack Property · Landscape URL Nova Guide
9

Systems submission — 10 business days

After the kickoff, the candidate has 10 business days to submit the 2 systems they want to move forward with, using the submission form in their email. The moment the form comes in, the team is notified so the analysis can begin.

Candidate form · 2 systems Automatic Slack
10

FDD & financial analysis

With the systems in, the team runs the FDD and financial analysis on them. Once it's complete, the Nova Guide schedules the FDD / financial analysis call to walk the candidate through what we found and talk through how to move forward.
