About This List

This is an early-stage landscape of franchise systems that line up with your two areas of interest — Home Services and Hospitality (extended-stay) — and with what you told us you’re looking for: recurring revenue, a model you can run semi-absentee through a manager, and room to grow into multiple units across the Atlanta metro. The home-services options are the most actionable near-term; the extended-stay entry is included because you flagged hospitality, with a note on what it really takes to pursue it.

Est. Investment = the total cost to open one location (franchise fee + build-out/equipment + working capital) — not what you earn. Figures are estimates from publicly available sources and vary by territory and FDD year.
